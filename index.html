About

Data with
purpose.

I'm a public health professional with an MPH and CPH certification, and 5+ years of experience leading data quality assurance operations on national-scale datasets. At BroadStreet Institute, I served as a QA Regional Lead during the COVID-19 response — designing integrity protocols, building exception reports, and delivering accurate data to partners across the country.

My research roots are in global health equity, with a focus on child and maternal health in India. I build data products that translate the invisible into the visible — giving decision-makers the evidence they need to act.

Currently based in Trenton, NJ (relocating to Orlando, FL). Open to fully remote roles in public health analytics, clinical quality improvement, and data integrity.
